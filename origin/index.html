Örigin

Founded in 2015, ÖNY is for the contemporary urban culture french high end streetwear new reference.

Breaking once and for all with hierarchy of art, the garment is back under the spotlight.

Rethought as a lasting aestetical creation, it's enhanced with multiple artistic collaborations.

Londoner by heart, Audrey Jouanny the young creator with eclectic inspiration offers an « urban smart » closet in which the diversification of the teddy jacket is endless.

For this 5th collection the basics of the streetwear closet are reinterpreted and taken to an even more futuristic universe.

Reversibility, asymmetry and oversized proportions are this season’s watchwords.

The teddy jacket, ÖNY’s centerpiece since the beginning, wipes off gender and gives a unisex tone.

From the connexion between man and woman lines flows an hybrid closet.

The use of carefully chosen materials made in France overcomes the groundbreaking volumes and cuts.

The wax is skillfully distilled, like a signature in the blue color chart. Acid prints are mixed with denim, chambray and poplin to give birth to a neophyte pantone : BLEU CITRON
